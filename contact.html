FAQ

Can I cancel my order?

Orders can only be canceled within 1 hour of placement. After this window, the order enters the fulfillment process and cannot be modified.

What if my item arrives damaged?

If you receive a defective or incorrect item, please email us immediately with photos of the issue and your order number. We will prioritize a replacement or a full refund.

Will the graphics fade?

We use high-quality printing and embroidery techniques designed to last. Following the care instructions above will ensure your pieces maintain their look for a long time.

How should I wash my hoodies and sweatpants?

To preserve the quality and fit of our premium fabrics, we recommend washing items inside out in cold water. Always air dry or tumble dry on the lowest heat setting to prevent shrinkage and protect any graphics.

What is your return policy?

We accept returns within 14 days of delivery. Items must be in their original, unworn condition with all tags attached. Please note that shipping costs for returns are the responsibility of the customer.

How do I start a return or exchange?

To begin a return, please contact our support team with your order number. We will provide you with the necessary instructions and the return shipping address.
